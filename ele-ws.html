Electrical Workshop

List of Experiments:

    1. Familiarization of wiring tools, lighting and wiring accessories, various types of wiring systems.
    2. Wiring of one lamp controlled by one switch.
    1. Study of Electric shock phenomenon, precautions, preventions, Earthing.
    2. Wiring of one lamp controlled by two SPDT Switches and one 3 pin plug socket independently.
    1. Familiarization of various types of Fuses, MCBs, ELCBs, etc.
    2. Wiring of fluorescent lamp controlled by one switch with ELCB & MCB.
    1. Study of estimation and costing of wiring.
    2. Wiring, control and maintenance of domestic appliances like Mixer machine, Electric Iron, fan, motor, etc.

References:

  1. K. B. Raina & S. K. Bhattacharya, Electrical Design Estimating and costing, New Age International Publishers, New Delhi, 2005.
  2. Uppal S. L., Electrical Wiring & Estimating, Khanna Publishers---5th edition, 2003.
  3. John H. Watt, Terrell Croft American Electricians' Handbook: A Reference Book for the Practical Electrical Man, 9th ed. McGraw-Hill, 2002.
